Tennessee Onions

Description

Tennessee onions are a delicious and unique dish that showcases the sweet and savory flavors of caramelized onions. This recipe is perfect as a side dish or a topping for your favorite meats.

Ingredients

  • 4 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, melt the butter and olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the sliced onions and sugar, stirring to coat.
  3. Cook the onions, stirring occasionally, until they are caramelized and golden brown, about 20-25 minutes.
  4. Season with salt and pepper, then add the balsamic vinegar.
  5. Cook for an additional 5 minutes, allowing the vinegar to reduce slightly.
  6. Remove from heat and garnish with fresh parsley before serving.
